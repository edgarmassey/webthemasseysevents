2026 is knocking on our door!! ...

As other years have always given us, this coming year will also doublessly offer many wonderful things  ... beautiful things from the best part of humanity within and around us --- warm and soft expression of kindness, understanding and thoughtfulness for others   ---  precious moments with those we love most ..  new expansions of the amazing treasures already in our lives  etc etc.  We must freely open our inner door to such gifts in store for us 2026.

The backside of every year -- chaos, conflicts and ugly moral betrayal by people and politics around us also seem to be a significant part of each year as it passes. We must see to it that these horrible things live and fester somewhere else than within our hearts. Not ignored, but perhaps best kept in the courtyard just outside the windows of our personal lives.

Happy New Year 2026
